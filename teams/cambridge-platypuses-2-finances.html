How Finances Work

Revenue

  • Per win +$12.8M — every dollar is earned on the court
  • Playoff berth +$15M · Finals +$15M · Title +$20M

Bonuses stack once clinched — the champion banks +$50M.

Tax & Budget

  • Luxury tax $1 per $1 of payroll over the $300M soft cap
  • Collected tax is split equally among under-cap teams
  • Net revenue (revenue − tax + tax share) is the whole next-season budget — no carried cash

League-average budget is $299M, just under the cap.
